The Crisis of Dialogue Today

Introduction

Dialogue has always been difficult: human beings have always faced conflict, prejudice, and trouble listening. However, today this difficulty has intensified. We live in a society where, despite having more tools to communicate, we struggle more than ever to truly understand one another.

Thesis: Today, listening to others is harder because modern factors—especially social media and post-pandemic isolation—amplify problems that have always existed.
